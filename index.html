Programming is more than just code writing

I started studying programming when I was 13 years old. At that time, I was interested in the creation of sites on Wordpress and DLE. The idea of ​​creating my online cinema or a book portal did not leave me for months.

A lot of playing with the engines, I decided to thoroughly approach this issue thoroughly. At the courses I studied HTML5 and CSS3 at a good level. Well it turned out to make small sites, but without the use of JS.

Over time, I felt the need for a javascript. So began a fascinating study of my first programming language. I also continued to make websites, studying and applying the language capabilities I studied in parallel. The jquery were replaced by a react and a redux - very convenient instruments. Together with them, git, webpack and other tools for the frontend. I did not confine myself to the frontend, it was interesting to try myself on the part of developing a server.

After some time I wanted to try myself in real projects and went to freelancing. One of the most interesting projects is the server on node.js for the photo studio management system. I worked on this project for a month.

Freelance became small and I decided to find a job in the company. It was not difficult, despite my age, the lack of a lot of experience and specific working conditions.
